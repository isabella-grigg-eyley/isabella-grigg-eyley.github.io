Cultural Blog, Sprint 5: Self Confidence

27 July, 2018
What is the relationship between mindfulness and self awareness?

They are very similar concepts. Both have definitions from specialists in each area that are very similar in description, and we already know that mindfulness can be trained, meaning self-awareness can also be trained. Mindfulness also leads to self-awareness: mindfulness leads to being conscious of one's emotions, which is part one of being self-aware.

Try at least one of the self awareness practices from the chapter (Body Scan, Journaling). What was your experience of it? How did you feel?

I did the body scan practice. He says afterwards that the body scan exercise tends to conduce sleep- which was definitely my biggest takeaway! I actually ended it a couple minutes earlier than I was meant to, just because I was so close to falling asleep. I will try it again though the next time I do a formal meditation practice (when I'm not already tired!). I did notice some tension I wasn't aware of previously, as well as how that changed slightly when I thought of a positive emotionally charged memory.

What did you take away from the "My Emotions are Not Me" section?

I really like the concept and think it can be helpful to frame emotions as a separate thing we can observe and react to of our own rational choosing, rather than being controlled by directly. I will try to bear this in mind in future.
